Cisco Enterprise Networking — EoL Tech Refresh for an IT Giant Across 36 Locations During the Pandemic

VIS Content Creation Team 18 October 2024 2 min read

Overview

In the fast-evolving landscape of enterprise networking, a remarkable transformation unfolded as an IT giant undertook a monumental project — the End-of-Life (EoL) Tech Refresh for Cisco Enterprise Networking. This ambitious endeavour aimed at migrating over 650 Cisco gears across 36 locations, signalling a major leap in technological evolution. As the end client, they engaged AT&T as a pivotal dealmaker, shaping a partnership that would redefine their networking landscape.

Challenges

The project unfolded in two meticulously planned phases. The first laid the groundwork for what was to come. Notably, the second phase unfolded against the backdrop of the COVID-19 pandemic — putting the resilience of the project team to the ultimate test.

Key Highlights

Geographic SpreadCovering a staggering 36 locations across India, this migration was a logistical and technical symphony on a nationwide scale — underscoring the complexity and magnitude of the deployment.
Seamless Migration Amidst ChallengesDespite the intricacies of migrating such a vast network, the transition from aging Cisco devices to new infrastructure was executed with finesse — even as the second phase unfolded during the pandemic.
Proper Planning & ExecutionPre-migration assessments, risk evaluations and contingency planning formed the backbone of a project executed with surgical precision and minimal disruption to operations.
Strategic Vendor RelationshipVIS Networks emerged as a key player — a strategic partnership crucial to the successful execution of the migration.

Results & Future Outlook

In the annals of enterprise networking, this EoL Tech Refresh stands out as a testament to effective project management, technical proficiency, and the resilience to thrive even in the face of unforeseen challenges.

Beyond a mere upgrade, it represents a milestone in the ever-evolving landscape of network infrastructure — setting a precedent for excellence in execution and adaptability in the IT industry.
