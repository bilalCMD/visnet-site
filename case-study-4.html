Network & Security

Modernizing Network Infrastructure with Cisco DNA Center and Software-Defined Access (SDA)

VIS Content Team 27 October 2024 2.5 min read

Overview

A significant stride towards the future of networking unfolded with the deployment of Cisco DNA Center for Software-Defined Access (SDA) — a strategic move representing a commitment to modernizing and optimizing the network infrastructure across key locations in India and the United States.

Key Highlights

Cisco DNA CenterThe linchpin of the deployment — a centralized network management and automation solution providing a unified dashboard to streamline operations, fortify security and introduce automation through Software-Defined Networking (SDN).
Software-Defined Access (SDA)Simplified network segmentation and elevated security through a policy-driven approach, shifting from manual configurations to network policies based on business intent — flexible, agile and scalable.
Strategic Client DeploymentA commitment to adopting cutting-edge networking technologies in a pivotal urban landscape.

Benefits

Enhanced SecurityMicro-segmentation isolated and controlled access to different segments based on policies, making the network inherently more resilient to security threats.
Automation & OrchestrationAutomation reduced manual configuration, streamlined provisioning and elevated operational efficiency — tasks that once demanded intricate manual inputs now flow seamlessly.
ScalabilityThe deployment positioned the network to scale gracefully as demands grew, adapting to the evolving technological landscape.
Policy-Driven NetworkingManagement aligned more closely with business goals — a key asset in navigating a dynamic, diverse network environment.

Conclusion

The deployment of Cisco DNA Center with SDA marks a strategic leap towards a more agile, secure and automated network infrastructure.

Aligned with a dynamic, critical urban environment, this initiative positions the network to meet evolving connectivity needs and sets a standard for integrating advanced networking technologies in complex landscapes.
