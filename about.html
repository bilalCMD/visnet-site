About VIS

From humble beginnings to transformative milestones — meet the people, vision and presence behind VIS Networks.

Our Story

A family of 500+ professionals

VIS Networks, founded in 2011 in Bangalore, India, is a leading provider across multiple business verticals.

  • Since inception, VIS has grown into a family of 500+ highly skilled & experienced technical professionals serving customers across the world — including Fortune 100 & Fortune 500 companies.
  • Offices in 10+ major cities of India with a global presence in the UK, USA, UAE, Oman, Singapore, Philippines, Malaysia & Australia.
  • We also serve Africa, China and Europe through our associate partners.
  • Strategic partnerships with the industry's leading & innovative organizations across the globe, offering a complete portfolio of end-to-end, performance-driven and highly reliable solutions.

Vision & Mission

Guided by a bold vision

Our Vision

To be the foremost leader in delivering unique, effective and reliable solutions that empower businesses to thrive in the digital age.

We aspire to transform the way organizations connect with their customers, partners and employees — setting new standards for excellence across industry verticals.

To be the driving force behind company excellence, helping organizations harness the power of technology to build stronger connections and achieve their goals.

Our Mission

At the heart of our mission lies a dedication to providing comprehensive services. We are trusted partners — ready to consult, design tailored solutions, implement them seamlessly, and provide training and ongoing support.

Our commitment is to continuously innovate, pushing the boundaries of technology to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

We aim to be recognized for our solutions and for our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity and customer-centricity.
