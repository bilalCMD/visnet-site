Network & Security

Juniper Mist AI Campus Network with EVPN-VXLAN Integration

VIS Content Creation Team 27 November 2024 3 min read

Background

A new venture under the Tata Group umbrella, focusing on precision component manufacturing in collaboration with Apple Inc., undertook a pioneering migration at its Hosur campus in Tamil Nadu — evolving a traditional network into a cutting-edge Juniper Campus network enriched with Mist AI technology.

Overview

Initiated in November 2022, the project introduced Juniper's Mist AI technology and EVPN-VXLAN for a comprehensive and efficient wired and wireless networking solution, restructuring the existing traditional network for the modern enterprise.

Challenges

The principal challenge was addressing complexity accumulated during the plant's initial phases — reducing it while adhering to global networking standards for security, agility and applicability, and maintaining network security with ease of management and scalability.

Key Highlights

Global Technological BreakthroughA global milestone — the first full-scale Mist-AI campus network solution for Juniper devices worldwide, delivering an efficient and scalable enterprise network with consistency.
Solution & Device IntegrationMigration across 6 buildings on a 50-acre campus, with a tailored Juniper Campus Switching Solution covering wired and wireless via EVPN-VXLAN through Mist AI.
Seamless CollaborationA smooth, seamless transition from old Cisco devices to new Juniper devices, underscoring strong communication and teamwork.
OT/IT SegregationSuccessful segregation of OT (Production) and IT networks — a challenging task given the project's scale and geographical spread.

Results & Future Outlook

With improved operational efficiencies, heightened security and a scalable foundation, the client is poised for continued success — including its prestigious project with Apple Inc.

The deployment demonstrates the adaptability and compatibility of Juniper's advanced networking solutions with diverse hardware ecosystems, setting a precedent for future innovation.
