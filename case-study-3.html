Pioneering Migration to EVPN-VXLAN Technology

VIS Content Creation Team 01 November 2024 2.5 min read

Background

In the dynamic realm of networking, the client achieved a monumental milestone with the successful completion of a pioneering migration project — transitioning from a traditional network to cutting-edge EVPN-VXLAN technology within the enterprise network.

Overview

The project unfolded across a strategic timeframe spanning 2021–22, and encompassed four key locations — Pune, Mumbai and Chennai in India, and Cary in the United States.

Key Highlights

Global Technological BreakthroughThe client became the first in the world to implement EVPN-VXLAN technology designed specifically for Juniper devices within an enterprise network — a mastery of cutting-edge networking complexities.
Juniper Device IntegrationTailoring the solution for Juniper devices added uniqueness, demonstrating the adaptability and compatibility of EVPN-VXLAN with diverse hardware ecosystems.
Strategic Network ImpactImproved scalability, increased efficiency and enhanced flexibility position the client for a more agile and responsive network.
Geographical DiversityNavigating locations from India (Pune, Mumbai, Chennai) to the USA (Cary) showcased the team's ability to handle the challenges of geographical diversity.
Collaboration with ClientEffective collaboration between the implementation team and the client's team was pivotal — a strong partnership built on communication and teamwork.
Setting Industry StandardsAs trailblazers implementing EVPN-VXLAN for Juniper devices in an enterprise network, the client set industry standards and positioned itself as a leader.

Conclusion

The successful migration to EVPN-VXLAN marks a groundbreaking achievement that transcends technical prowess — exemplifying a commitment to innovation and staying at the forefront of networking advancements.

As the first to tread this path, the client emerges not just as a network transformer but as a beacon guiding the future of enterprise networking.
